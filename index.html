About Us

At Crystal Crafter, we believe every home deserves a touch of elegance and every person deserves a hint of sparkle. We are an online store dedicated to offering a curated collection of exquisite glass bangles, decorative items, vases, and lamps — all handcrafted with passion and precision.

Our mission is to support local artisans and preserve the rich tradition of glass and crystal craftsmanship. Each product is thoughtfully designed to blend timeless beauty with modern taste, making it the perfect gift or home décor piece.
